Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use ExpenseIQ, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when registering, along with the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the Service: authenticating your account, storing your expenses, and generating the summaries and category breakdowns you see in the app. We do not use your personal expense data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

ExpenseIQ does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or email delivery services) solely to operate the Service, and only to the extent necessary.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
